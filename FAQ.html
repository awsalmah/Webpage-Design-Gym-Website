Why join Arnie's?

Here at Arnie's we offer the very best services possible in all of our departments, alongisde the best equipment for your training.


How much does a membership cost?

Membership prices vary by the package chosen. To Find prices for each membership tier kindly visit the memberships page.


How can I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership at any time by giving us a 30 day written notice from your next Direct Debit collection date provided you have completed the payment of your current membership.


How can I freeze my membership?

To hold your current membership you can either contact us by phone or request it be frozen in person.
Please note that there is a fee of US$30 to freeze an account.


How can I unfreeze my membership?

To reactivate your current membership simply enter the gym and use the fingerprint scanner to access the gym's facilities.


How many days are left for my current membership?

To view the remaining amount of time for your membership, log into your account and click on my membership.


What is a Guest Pass and how does it work?

Guest Passes are virtual tickets added to your account whenever you buy/renew your membership.
They act by allowing you to bring an acquaintance with you free of charge to the gym for a day.
To use a Guest Pass, please contact our front desk when entering the gym.
Please note that Guest Passes are only valid if the member is present.


What are some of the privileges of a guest?

Guests have the same privileges as members.


How many Guest Passes do I have?

The number of Guest Passes a member has depends on their membership. To view the number of Guest Passes you have log in to your account.


How do I reset my password?

You can either log into your account and change your password from the myaccount page or you can contact us if you are unable to log in to your account.
